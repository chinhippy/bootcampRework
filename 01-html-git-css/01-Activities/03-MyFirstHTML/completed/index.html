Steps to Making a Sandwich

  1. Gather ingredients: 2 slices of bread, mayo, turkey, lettuce, 2 tomato slices.
  2. Gather equipment: toaster, butter knife, plate.
  3. Place the two slices of bread in toaster. Toast to desired doneness.
  4. Once toast is done. Place both pieces on plate and spread desired amount of mayo to both pieces using butter knife.
  5. Place turkey, tomatoes, and lettuce on one piece of toast.
  6. Place second piece of toast ontop of ingredients.
  7. Enjoy!
